\section{Software}
See the \href{https://chadhazlett.com/software.html}{software} section of my website for current details.
\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{sensemakr} --- Sensitivity of regression results to unobserved confounding. R: \texttt{install.packages("sensemakr")}; Stata: \texttt{ssc install sensemakr}. \href{https://github.com/carloscinelli/sensemakr}{GitHub}.
\item \texttt{kbal} --- Kernel balancing, including the \texttt{kpop()} wrapper for survey reweighting. R: \texttt{install.packages("kbal")}. \href{https://github.com/chadhazlett/KBAL}{GitHub}.
\item \texttt{gpss} --- Gaussian processes for social science (regression, RD, interrupted time series). R: \texttt{install.packages("gpss")}. \href{https://doeunkim.org/gpss/}{Docs}.
\item \texttt{scqe} --- Stability-controlled quasi-experiment. R: \texttt{install.packages("scqe")}; \href{https://amiwulf.shinyapps.io/SCQE_demo/}{web app}.
\item \texttt{KRLS} --- Kernel-regularized least squares. R: \texttt{install.packages("KRLS")}; Stata: \texttt{ssc install krls}.
\item \texttt{pERPred} --- Principle ERP reduction and analysis. \texttt{devtools::install\_github("emjcampos/pERPred")}.
\end{itemize}
